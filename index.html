The person behind the practice

A combination most companies do not yet have a title for.

Most advisors have lived one side of this work. Tom has lived three: Deep AI product, global consulting, and international HR operations. The category is new enough that the role keeps being renamed. The imperative is not.

Tom Delaporte founded TD Catalyst after more than a decade at the intersection of Deep AI product, global consulting, and international HR operations. Today, the practice advises the leaders building, buying, and adopting AI at work.

Before founding TD Catalyst, Tom spent over five years at Eightfold AI, leading Talent Transformation, Talent Intelligence Platform, and Agentic Scale programs. He built the public sector practice from a standing start into an eight-figure ARR business, across Fortune 100 and public sector clients. Earlier, as an Engagement Manager at Capgemini Invent in San Francisco, he led organizational design, change, and M&A integration engagements for global companies — including a 2.5 billion USD pharmaceutical post-merger integration. Before consulting, he ran HR and transformation programs on the operator side, across France, Vietnam, the Philippines, and the United States.

The result is a perspective that is genuinely rare: product-led and operator-sharp on the founder side, enterprise-seasoned and people-fluent on the adoption side, with commercial accountability and multicultural fluency throughout. Bilingual French and English, the ability to read people across cultures is not a credential on a slide — it is how the work actually gets done.
