Thomas Delaporte founded TD Catalyst after more than a decade at the intersection of AI product, global consulting, and HR operations. Today, the practice advises the leaders building, buying, and adopting agentic AI at work.

Before founding TD Catalyst, Thomas spent over five years at Eightfold AI, leading Talent Transformation, Talent Intelligence Platform, and Agentic Scale programs. He built the public sector and enterprise practice from a standing start into an eight-figure ARR business, across Fortune 100 and public sector clients. Earlier, as an Engagement Manager at Capgemini Invent in San Francisco, he led organizational design, change, and M&A integration engagements for global companies — including a 2.5 billion USD pharmaceutical post-merger integration. Before consulting, he ran HR and transformation programs on the operator side, across France, Vietnam, the Philippines, and the United States.

The result is a perspective that is genuinely rare: product-led and operator-sharp on the founder side, enterprise-seasoned and people-fluent on the adoption side, with commercial accountability and multicultural fluency throughout. Bilingual French and English, the ability to read people across cultures is not a credential on a slide — it is how the work actually gets done.
