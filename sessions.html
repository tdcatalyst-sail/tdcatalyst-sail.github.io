Four ways to engage. One recommended starting point.

Most work begins on the Pacifica coast. The setting is deliberate. It changes the conversation, and it changes what leaders are willing to say out loud. Pricing ranges below are directional. Final scope is set after a 30 minute call.

Trail Diagnostic
Half day · Mori Point · Pacifica

A single session designed to name the specific gap between an AI initiative and the workforce reality it needs to work inside. Fit for enterprise leaders with a stalled adoption, and for founders seeking enterprise readiness.

  • Pre read and intake questionnaire
  • Four hour working session on the trail
