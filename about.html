About

A combination most companies do not yet have a title for.

Most advisors have lived one side of AI workforce transformation. Tom has lived three: Deep AI product, global consulting, and international HR operations. The category is new enough that the role keeps being renamed. The imperative is not.

Practice

Tom Delaporte founded TDcatalyst after more than a decade at the intersection of Deep AI product, global consulting, and international HR operations. Today, the practice advises the leaders building, buying, and adopting AI at work.

The typical client is a mid to large enterprise transformation, AI, or workforce leader with accountability for how AI enters the operating model. Founders seeking enterprise readiness are welcome and often find the diagnostic session a fit.

Background

Before founding TDcatalyst, Tom spent over five years at Eightfold AI, leading Talent Transformation, Talent Intelligence Platform, and Agentic Scale programs. He built the public sector practice from a standing start into an eight figure ARR business, across Fortune 100 and public sector clients.

Earlier, as an Engagement Manager at Capgemini Invent in San Francisco, he led organizational design, change, and M and A integration engagements for global companies, including a 2.5 billion USD pharmaceutical post merger integration. Before consulting, he ran HR and transformation programs on the operator side, across France, Vietnam, the Philippines, and the United States.

The result is a perspective that is genuinely rare: product led and operator sharp on the founder side, enterprise seasoned and people fluent on the adoption side, with commercial accountability and multicultural fluency throughout. Bilingual French and English. The ability to read people across cultures is not a credential on a slide. It is how the work actually gets done.

Why Pacifica

The sessions begin on the Pacifica coast because the setting changes the conversation. Mori Point, Timigtac Trail, Calera Creek, and the nearby redwood stands sit at the edge of the continent, and at the edge of how people usually talk about their work. Leaders say things on a trail they would not say in a conference room. That is part of the method.

The Aramai band of the Ramaytush Ohlone people have stewarded this land for thousands of years. TDcatalyst operates on their ancestral territory and pays an annual Yunakin Land Tax in acknowledgment.
